About NaviTagi

 What’s the goal behind NaviTagi?

The goal is to make it simple and easy to return your pet home, and to obtain that security as soon as possible. So all you need to do is go to a store, pick the right color and size for your NaviTagi, tie your contact data to the tag’s ID number online, and put it on your pet right away!

 Always up-to-date contact data

The pet might run away at any point, and the best way to deal with such an unfortunate situation is to be prepared. You need to keep your contact data up to date to ensure you can be reached when someone finds your pet. NaviTagi allows you to do exactly that - you can update your contact information at any moment, all online. Even when you made an unexpected trip and the pet ran away - you can change your contact information to insure your pet’s safe return home.

 WEB based, NO fees!

Registration, view, and editing of contact information all are done online at www.ids.pet:
-The site is specifically designed to be mobile-friendly
-No application needs to be downloaded, so you can save your phone’s memory
-No need to create or remember logins and passwords
-All actions on www.ids.pet are free - no subscription or hidden fees .
