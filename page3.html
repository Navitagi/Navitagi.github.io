FAQs

Do I need to pay a subscription fee once I buy NaviTagi?

-Never. You only pay once, when you buy NaviTagi tag. All actions (registration, view, editing of contact information, and other) are free throughout your use of NaviTagi.

How many NaviTagi tags can I tie to my phone number?

-As many as you would like. You can tie any number of NaviTagi to the same contact information. 

How do I change my contact information?

-It is very easy. Go to “Change data” on corresponding website and enter your current phone number. We will send a confirmation code to your phone number and to your email address. Enter this code when prompted on the website and after that you will be able to modify your contact data.

 I lost my phone (my number changed). What do I do?

-Go to “Change data” on corresponding website, and enter the phone number you previously used (the one that you lost or would like changed). We will send a confirmation code to your email address that you saved when registering NaviTagi. Enter this code when prompted on the website and after that you will be able to modify your contact data.

 NaviTagi got dirty - can I wash it?

-Yes, the tag is very easy to clean and maintain. Take off the metal ring, put NaviTagi in a washing bag (or simply in a sock!) and wash with the rest of your laundry (preferably with dark or colored laundry and temperature below 60°C / 140°F).
